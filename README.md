# 🏛️ El Greco | The Estate
**By Andrew Mikeladze**

This is the private digital headquarters for the El Greco Estate in Batumi. A fusion of Hellenic heritage and Georgian shores.

## 📜 The Concept
El Greco is more than a restaurant—it is an estate dedicated to the philosophy of **Philoxenia**: the Greek art of making a stranger feel like a brother.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
* **HTML5** - Semantic structure for the culinary journey.
* **CSS3** - Custom styling featuring "Greek Blue" and "Estate Gold" accents.
* **JavaScript** - Multi-language support (EN, GE, EL) for a global audience.

## 🏗️ Architecture
* **Responsive Design:** Optimized for every screen, from mobile devices to high-end desktops.
* **Interactive Menu:** Signature items from Chios and the Estate Bar.
* **Reservation System:** Integrated booking interface.
