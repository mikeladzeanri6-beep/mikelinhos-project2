Two Cultures, One Soul

Andrew Mikeladze is a man of two worlds. Born in the historic heart of Athens and raised amidst the fragrant citrus groves and mastic trees of Chios, Andrew has lived the authentic Hellenic experience from birth.

Now, Andrew brings that heritage to the shores of Batumi. El Greco is more than a restaurant—it is an estate dedicated to the philosophy of Philoxenia: the Greek art of making a stranger feel like a brother.
